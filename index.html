How to Play

1

Swipe to Move

Swipe up, down, left, or right to move all tiles in that direction. New tiles appear with each move.

2

Merge Same Numbers

When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one tile with double the value! Create a chain of merges for higher scores.

3

Use Power-Ups

Strategically use Swap, Undo, Bomb, and Freeze power-ups to overcome difficult situations. Purchase more with coins you earn.

4

Complete Challenges

Take on daily challenges to earn coins and test your skills across different game modes with unique objectives.

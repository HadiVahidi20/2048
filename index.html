Game Features

Classic 2048 Gameplay

Merge matching numbers to create larger values. Can you reach the 2048 tile and beyond?

Power-Ups

Use special abilities like Swap, Undo, Bomb, and Freeze to overcome challenging situations.

Daily Challenges

Test your skills with daily challenges across different difficulty levels and earn rewards.

Multiple Board Sizes

Choose between 4×4, 5×5, or 6×6 boards for different levels of challenge.

In-Game Economy

Earn coins as you play to purchase power-ups and enhance your gameplay experience.

Light & Dark Themes

Choose between light and dark themes for comfortable gameplay day or night.

How to Play

1

Swipe to Move

Swipe up, down, left, or right to move all tiles in that direction.

2

Merge Same Numbers

When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one tile with double the value!

3

Use Power-Ups

Strategically use power-ups to overcome difficult situations and reach higher scores.

4

Complete Challenges

Take on daily challenges to earn coins and test your skills across different game modes.

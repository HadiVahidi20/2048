How to Play

1

Swipe to Move

Swipe up, down, left, or right to move all tiles on the board in that direction.

2

Merge Same Numbers

When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one tile with doubled value.

3

Use Power-Ups

Collect and use special power-ups to overcome challenging situations.

4

Reach 2048

Create a tile with the number 2048 to win! Can you go even further?
